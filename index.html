Staff

We have highly skilled staff who are very official and are are ready to tend to the needs of any client that visits our offices. The company ensures the safety and well being of the staff thus the stuff work whole heartedly ensuring quality services to our customers.

Partnershpis

The company partners with other award winning companies to ensure customer satisfaction. The company has branches in Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru, Kisumu, Machakos, Kiambu and Kirinyaga with its headquaters in Ukunda, Kwale county. Other branches are being constructed in various parts of the country.
